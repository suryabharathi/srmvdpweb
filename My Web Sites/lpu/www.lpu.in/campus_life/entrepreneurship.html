University Initiatives

Helping you StartUP!

Incubation centres

University provides office space & equipment, technology support and seed capital to nurture the upcoming entrepreneurial talents for viable projects. Thus making it more robust and approachable for the individual who has high ambition.

Mentoring

University has dedicated Business Development mentors to assist students who are coming with the business ideas and to provide entrepreneurship training. The guidance given by mentors plays an important role to start a venture as they have to check the feasibility of the business.

Associations

University has industry associates giving one on one networking and self-employment tips to establish as successful entrepreneurs. Associations trigger confidence in students to take individual decision and to start their business plans.

Capacity building programs

University counts the importance of activities closely linked to capacity building by conducting skill development events like hackathon, start up showcase, Technical guest lectures, Industry interactions and panel discussions.

Indian Society of New Era Engineers

ISNEE provides opportunity to diploma & graduate engineers to pursue their technical expertise. Venture offers compact training sessions on designing Go Karts and Formula cars where candidates perform in groups.

Imperial Society of Innovative Engineers

ISIE delivers industrial live project training which assist students to develop and improve their automobile engineering skills. Handling technical situations from scratch to final product is the strength of this venture.

Finesse

A Social Media Market venture that brings quality learning solutions to corporates and educational sectors by offering digital consulting services. It appeared as a 360 degree brand practice for social media & digital consulting area.

Pink Hand Technologies

A software development company which provides high-tech IT solutions with accuracy. A flexible corporation whose goal line is to carry transparency with flexibility in software development as per client requirement.

Dry Tickets

Australias most trusted online box office. An exclusive way to reach out audience by helping them in searching and buying live event tickets online. Company has few major processes in progress for online clients.

GridLocate

GridLocate develops GPS enabled solutions that allow individual to locate anyone anywhere at anytime. This venture provides the service in easy to install steps and with accurate result which makes its user more friendly.
