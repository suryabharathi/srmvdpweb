Career Options in Japanese language:

  • Interpreter: It involves the conversion of conversation in Japanese to English and vice-versa.
  • Translator: The profile requires the candidate to convert written documents from Japanese to English and vice-versa.
  • IT and Automobile Companies: Many Japanese Companies and Indian Companies prefer candidates with Technical Knowledge along with Japanese Skills as most of their clients are Japanese.
  • Work in Japan: After learning the advanced level of Japanese, the opportunities are open for candidates to work in Japan.
  • Outbound Process: There are several BPOs which are dealing with Japanese Clients and they look for candidates who can provide them Language support.
  • Teaching: There are opportunities for those who want to add value by teaching Japanese in Schools, Colleges, Institution and Corporate Offices.
  • Hospitality Industry: Candidates can also look for their career option in several Travel Companies and Hotels and Many more.

Career Options in French, German and Spanish language:

  • Translation: Medical/Literature texts, websites, official documents etc.
  • Interpretation: At seminars & conferences.
  • Teaching: At Schools, Universities, Institutes, Language academies and Companies
  • Jobs in embassies: Foreign Embassies in India and Indian embassies abroad
  • India’s foreign services: Jobs at Ministry of External Affairs
  • Publishing: News, Periodicals, Books, directories etc.
  • Tourism: As a tour guide/operator in India or abroad, Travel agent etc.
  • Entertainment: Dubbing/Subtitles, works related to cinema, theatre, advertising etc.
  • Business and marketing: international business/trade, Import/Export etc.)
  • Public relations and media: Jobs with TV channels, newspapers, journals etc.

Career Options in Chinese Language:

  • Interpreter: It involves the conversion of conversation in Chinese to English and vice-versa.
  • Translator: The profile requires the candidate to convert written documents from Chinese to English and vice-versa.
  • IT and Automobile Companies: Many Chinese Companies and Indian Companies prefer candidates with Technical Knowledge along with Chinese Skills as most of their clients are China.
  • Research Analyst: When it comes to large companies that have competition in countries like China and other Mandarin-speaking nations, having Chinese language skills are a major plus when investigating information that may not be available in English.
  • Work in China: After learning the advanced level of Chinese, the opportunities are open for candidates to work in China.
  • Outbound Process: There are several BPOs which are dealing with Chinese Clients and they look for candidates who can provide them Language support.
  • Teaching: There are opportunities for those who want to add value by teaching Chinese in Schools, Colleges, Institution and Corporate Offices.

Professional Speaking Skills

Gain speaking skill expertise in English and earn certification

Fluency and accuracy in speaking skills are a prerequisite for employability and professional success. Accordingly, a skill development program for improving oral communication skills has been designed in line with national requirements as outlined by the National Skills Development Corporation and other agencies. School of Professional Enhancement, Department of Soft Skills II is pleased to offer a short term Certificate Course in Professional Speaking Skills.

English Language

Gain expertise in English language and earn certification

Since many students feels the need of honing their English Language Skills for better placement or for higher studies; this course will provide the students a platform to improve their skills. The Department of Verbal Ability-IV under the flagship of the Centre of Professional Enhancement II, proposes to introduce English Language Classes. The programme “Certificate in English Language C1” is a course and duration of the course will be 120 hours and course will be divided into two equal halves of 60 hours each. Minimum student strength of the program will be 10 for each course.

This programme will be based on one-time Admission but Course Fee has to be taken Course wise and it cannot be carried forward. For the Students after first 60 hours exit option will be there and on successful completion first part with pass grade the students will be awarded an “Certificate in English Language B2” certificate.
